# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We — the maintainer, contributors, and AI agents working on KindredPaws — pledge to make
participation a harassment-free, kind, and constructive experience for everyone.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful, patient, and welcoming.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the project and its players.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discriminatory or derogatory comments, personal
attacks, and publishing others' private information without consent.

## Scope

This applies within all project spaces (issues, pull requests, discussions, commits) and in
public spaces when an individual is representing the project.

## Enforcement

Report concerns to the maintainer via a private GitHub Security advisory or email. The
maintainer will review and respond to all good-faith reports and may take any action deemed
appropriate, up to and including a ban from project spaces.
