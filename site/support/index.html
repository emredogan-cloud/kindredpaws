Common questions

“Is my pet safe if I change phones or reinstall?”

Your pet is saved on your device after every interaction. When cloud save is active on your account, it also syncs securely so signing in on a new device brings your friend home. If the app ever cannot read your save, it will never quietly replace your pet — you'll see a recovery screen with options, and a safety copy is kept on the device.

“How do I restore a purchase?”

Open the Forever Friends sheet and tap Restore purchases. Make sure you're signed into the same App Store / Google Play account you purchased with.

“How do I manage or cancel my subscription?”

Subscriptions are managed by your store account, not inside the app: App Store → your Apple ID → Subscriptions, or Google Play → Payments & subscriptions. Cancelling never affects your pet.

“How do I delete my data?”

Settings → Delete my data. This permanently erases your pet's save from the device and the cloud and unlinks analytics identifiers. It cannot be undone.

“Notifications arrive at odd times / not at all.”

Check Settings → Notifications inside the app, then your system notification permissions. KindredPaws sends at most two gentle notes per day and never guilt-trips.

Found a bug?

Email us, or if you're technical, open an issue on GitHub — it goes straight to the developer.
