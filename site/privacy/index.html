Privacy Policy

Effective date: 8 July 2026 · Applies to the KindredPaws mobile app for iOS and Android.

KindredPaws is a cozy virtual-pet game built to be child-safe for every player. We collect as little as the game needs to work, we never sell data, we never use your data to track you across other apps or websites, and you can erase everything from inside the app.

What we never collect

  • No location, contacts, photos, messages, browsing history, or health data.
  • No audio. If a voice feature ever ships, processing will be on-device only — audio never leaves your phone.
  • No free-form text from children: the single text field (your pet's name) is filtered before it is stored; the pet's "memories" come from a closed, pre-written set — never from anything a child typed.
  • No advertising identifiers used for behavioral or cross-app tracking, ever.

Children

KindredPaws is designed to a child-safe standard for all players: the pet's dialogue is pre-written and reviewed (no live chatbot), there is no user-to-user communication, no behavioral advertising, and nothing your pet needs can be harmed by not paying. Where the app is directed to children, we comply with COPPA and GDPR-K, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children beyond what this policy describes.

Where your data lives

Your pet lives on your device first. When cloud save is active, your save syncs to Google Firebase (Firestore) under your account identifier, encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+). Purchases are processed by Apple's App Store or Google Play and validated through RevenueCat; we never see your card details. We do not sell data, and we do not share it with data brokers.

Deletion — the right to be forgotten

Settings → "Delete my data" erases your pet's save from your device and our cloud, and unlinks analytics identifiers, immediately and permanently. Anonymized purchase records may be retained where the law requires financial record-keeping — with the personal link removed.

Your rights

Depending on where you live (GDPR, UK GDPR, CCPA/CPRA and similar), you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or port your data, and to object to processing. The in-app deletion flow covers erasure without needing to email anyone; for anything else, contact us below and we will respond within 30 days.

Changes

If this policy changes materially, we will update this page and note it in the app's release notes before the change takes effect.
