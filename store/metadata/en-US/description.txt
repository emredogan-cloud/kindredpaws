On a cold, rainy evening, a tiny shape is curled up all alone. You kneel down, reach out… and bring home a friend.

KindredPaws is a cozy virtual-pet game about rescuing a puppy or kitten, raising it from a wobbly little one into a grown companion, and building a real bond — with a pet that actually remembers you.

🐾 RESCUE & RAISE
Adopt your companion in a gentle 60-second cold-open, then care for it day by day — feed, clean, play. Watch it grow through life stages and blossom from a shy stranger into your Soulmate.

💛 A PET THAT REMEMBERS
Your companion learns small, true things about you and brings them up later — "I still think about how you like that." It greets you warmly when you return, comforts you on hard days, and celebrates your milestones. Never naggy, never guilt-trippy. Just warm.

🏡 COZY BY DESIGN
No timers that punish you for living your life. No "your pet is starving!" guilt. If you're away, your pet simply naps and saves you the warmest spot. A care streak that only ever stays warm.

🔒 SAFE & KIND FOR EVERYONE
Built child-safe for all players: no behavioral ad targeting, no free-text from kids, careful AI guardrails, and a pet whose wellbeing is never tied to your wallet. The pet can never get sick or be lost — it's a comfort object, by design.
